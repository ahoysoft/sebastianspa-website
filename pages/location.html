Beaches & Waterways

Sebastian offers some of Florida's most beautiful and uncrowded beaches

Sebastian Inlet State Park

Premier surf spot with excellent fishing, swimming, and wildlife viewing. One of Florida's top beaches.

Indian River Lagoon

One of the most biodiverse estuaries in North America. Perfect for kayaking, paddle boarding, and wildlife spotting.

Wabasso Beach

Quiet, family-friendly beach with calm waters. Great for swimming and beachcombing.

Pelican Island

America's first National Wildlife Refuge. Excellent for bird watching and eco-tours.

Day Trip Destinations

Explore beyond Sebastian - exciting destinations within easy driving distance

Kennedy Space Center

Experience space exploration history and current NASA missions. About 45 minutes north.

Vero Beach

Upscale beach town with excellent dining, shopping, and cultural attractions. Just 15 minutes south.

Melbourne

Historic downtown, Brevard Zoo, and great restaurant scene. About 30 minutes north.

Orlando Theme Parks

Disney World, Universal Studios, and SeaWorld are about 90 minutes west for a fun day trip.

Cocoa Beach

Famous surf town with the iconic Ron Jon Surf Shop and Cocoa Beach Pier. About 40 minutes north.

Fort Pierce

Historic fishing village, Navy SEAL Museum, and great seafood restaurants. About 25 minutes south.
