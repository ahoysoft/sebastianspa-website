Guest Information

Check-In & Check-Out

Arriving at Sebastian Spa Retreat

Check-In Details

Check-in Time: 4:00 PM

Access: Keyless entry system - no physical keys needed. You'll receive your access code before arrival through Airbnb messaging.

Parking: Park in the driveway. Up to 4 cars allowed.

The entire home is yours to enjoy, except the garage (which is closed) and a small outdoor shed.

Check-Out Details

Check-out Time: 11:00 AM

Before You Leave:

  • Remove all food from refrigerator
  • Load and start the dishwasher
  • Place used towels in the bathtub
  • Turn off all lights and TVs
  • Lock all doors when leaving

WiFi & Connectivity

Stay connected during your stay

High-Speed WiFi

The property has high-speed internet access throughout. WiFi network name and password will be provided in your welcome message on Airbnb.

Great for streaming, video calls, and working remotely!

Smart TVs

Multiple Smart TVs throughout the home. Log into your favorite streaming services (Netflix, Hulu, Disney+, etc.) for entertainment.

Remember to log out of your accounts before you leave!

Appliance & Feature Guides

How to use the amenities

Heated Pool

  • Pool is heated and ready to use
  • Pool toys in storage bench
  • Please shower before entering
  • No glass near pool area
  • Pool lights available at night

Hot Tub

  • Hot tub is ready for use
  • Cover should be replaced after use
  • Maximum 4 people recommended
  • NO candles, oils, or additives
  • Damage from additives = repair fee

Barrel Sauna

  • Located on the terrace
  • Allow 15-20 min to heat up
  • Temperature control inside
  • Stay hydrated - drink water
  • Turn off when finished

BBQ Grill

  • Propane BBQ grill included
  • Propane tank provided
  • Grilling tools in outdoor area
  • Please clean grates after use
  • Turn off propane when done

House Rules

Please respect these rules to ensure a great experience for everyone

General Rules

  • No Smoking - Smoking is not allowed inside the house or within the screened enclosure
  • Noise Levels - Be a courteous neighbor and keep noise to respectable levels at all times
  • Parking - Cars must be in the driveway only. No parking on yard, street, or at neighbors. Maximum 4 cars allowed
  • Security - Lock all doors when leaving the property

Hot Tub Rules

  • No Additives - Do NOT put candles, oils, bath bombs, or anything extra in the hot tub water
  • These substances can damage the nozzles and equipment
  • Repair Fee - If a guest uses prohibited substances, there will be a repair fee charged
  • Replace the cover after each use

Garbage & Recycling

Waste collection information

Green Bin - Garbage

Regular household waste goes in the green bin.

Blue Bin - Recyclables

Clean recyclables (plastic, paper, glass, aluminum) go in the blue bin.

Collection Day: Thursday

Please set bins at the road by the entryway the evening before (Wednesday night). Return bins after collection.
