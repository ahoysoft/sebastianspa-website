Local Events & Festivals

Sebastian hosts many exciting events throughout the year

Sebastian Clambake

Annual seafood festival featuring fresh clams, live music, and family fun. Usually held in November.

Pelican Island Wildlife Festival

Celebrate the first National Wildlife Refuge with eco-tours, exhibits, and educational programs.

Sebastian River Art Show

Fine arts and crafts festival featuring local and regional artists. Held at Riverview Park.

Friday Night Concerts

Free concerts at Riverview Park during season. Bring a blanket and enjoy live music by the river.

Farmer's Markets

Weekly farmer's markets offering fresh local produce, crafts, and homemade goods.

Fishing Tournaments

Various fishing tournaments throughout the year. Sebastian Inlet is a premier fishing destination.
