Bedroom and Bathroom 1 (master): king bed, dedicated working space with desk and chair, ample walk in closet and private bathroom. Access to the screened patio and hot tub. Bathroom essentials included. Smart TV 80” on wall.

Bedroom 2 (kids/guests): Two personal beds, foldable crib available in closet and stroller available upon request.
